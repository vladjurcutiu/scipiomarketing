Feast this Christmas at Restaurants in Downtown Dubai

Blog Post

Feasts and Christmas go hand in hand. They are traditionally linked, and they represent a time for families to bond and celebrate the end of the year. But imagine what a Christmas feast would look (and taste) like in one of the best restaurants in downtown Dubai.

At the Palace Downtown, you will dine in a rich, abundant setting, and you will be treated to a variety of both traditional and exotic dishes. If you are looking for a beverage, exclusive tea, or the best Arabian coffee of your life, the Al Bayt Lounge is perfect for you, with a scenic panoramic view, making it ideal in the evening as the sun sets and city lights up.

The Address Restaurants in Downtown Dubai

Asado is another great location for a Christmas dinner. What better way to spend Christmas than by eating steak and drinking premium beverages brewed according to Argentinian tradition, while in the heart of Dubai?

Or maybe you’d like to try some of the native cuisine at Ewaan. A luxurious restaurant draped in flamboyant accessories, Ewaan serves breakfast, lunch, dinner, and Friday brunch, with Oriental and Arabic dishes prepared in live cooking stations.

You then have the Buhayra Lounge, which delights guests with a mix of beverages, shisha, music and mezzeh. What really makes Buhayra stand is the magical setting. Guests will dine and drink in traditional Arabian tents by the pool - a true “Arabian Nights” experience.

If you prefer seafood, you have Thiptara, which is a romantic restaurant that serves Thai cuisine. The name of the restaurant means “Magic at the Water”, which is fitting due to the culinary style, and its location overlooking the Dubai Fountain.

Finishing The Night

Once you’ve dined at the finest restaurants in Downtown Dubai, the night does not need to end. Open until 2:00 AM, FAI is located on an “island” garden, which mixes fire and water to create a vibrant atmosphere. Serving amazing cocktails at the edge of Burj Khalifa, FAI is a great place to socialize and end a Christmas celebration.

Treat yourself this Christmas at one or more of Address’ prime restaurants, lounges and cocktail bars. A true feast awaits you, with the best food and drinks in the world.
